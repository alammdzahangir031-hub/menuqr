✅ Our goal is simple: if MenuQR doesn't work for your restaurant, we don't want your money. We'd rather give you a refund than have an unhappy customer.

1

Our Commitment

MenuQR operates on a subscription model. We believe in being transparent and fair about refunds. We are a small team based in Chittagong, Bangladesh, and we value every customer relationship.

This policy applies to all paid subscriptions purchased directly through MenuQR. If you subscribed through a third-party reseller or partner, their refund policy applies.

All amounts are in Bangladeshi Taka (BDT) unless otherwise specified.

2

Free Trial Policy

All MenuQR plans come with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start.

  • You will not be charged anything during the trial period
  • If you cancel before the trial ends, you will never be charged
  • If you do not cancel, your subscription automatically begins at the end of the trial and payment is collected
  • Once a paid billing cycle begins, the refund policy below applies

💡 Tip: Use the full 14 days to test MenuQR with your actual restaurant. We encourage this — real-world testing is the best way to know if it's right for you.

3

Monthly Plan Refunds

For monthly subscribers:

  • Within 7 days of payment: Full refund, no questions asked. If you signed up, were charged, and realized MenuQR isn't right for your restaurant within 7 days, we will refund you completely.
  • After 7 days: Refunds are not issued for the current billing period. You may cancel at any time and your access continues until the end of the paid period.
  • Technical failure: If MenuQR was completely unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours in a billing period due to our fault, we will issue a pro-rated credit or refund for those days.

⚠️ Monthly subscriptions are not refundable after the 7-day window except in cases of documented service failure on our end.

4

Annual Plan Refunds

For annual subscribers (who pay 12 months upfront at a 25% discount):

  • Within 14 days of payment: Full refund, no questions asked.
  • 15 days to 3 months: Pro-rated refund for the remaining unused months, minus a 10% processing fee.
  • After 3 months: No refund is issued. You retain access until the end of your annual period.

Example: If you pay for an annual plan and cancel after 2 months, you would receive a refund for the remaining 10 months minus the processing fee.

📌 Annual plans offer the best value. We recommend starting with a monthly plan first if you are unsure, then upgrading to annual once you are satisfied.

5

When Refunds Are Not Issued

We cannot issue refunds in the following situations:

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • Within 7 days of monthly payment
  • Within 14 days of annual payment
  • Proven service outage (>24 hrs)
  • Duplicate accidental charge
  • Charged after cancellation confirmed

❌ Not Eligible

  • Changed your mind after the window
  • Didn't use the service during the period
  • Account suspended for Terms violation
  • Forgot to cancel before renewal
  • Dissatisfied with a specific feature
  • Business closed or slow season

If you are unsatisfied with a specific feature, please contact us — we may be able to resolve your issue, add it to our roadmap, or find a workaround.

6

Service Issues & Credits

MenuQR aims for 99.5% uptime. If we fall short due to issues on our end, here is how we handle it:

  • Outage under 2 hours: No credit issued, but we will communicate transparently about the cause
  • Outage 2–24 hours: 1-week account credit added automatically
  • Outage over 24 hours: Pro-rated refund or credit for affected days, issued automatically within 7 days
  • WhatsApp delivery failures: If WhatsApp itself (Meta) experiences an outage, this is outside MenuQR's control and not eligible for refund. We will notify you of known WhatsApp issues.

Service credits are applied to your account and used against your next billing cycle. Credits cannot be converted to cash.

8

Processing & Timeline

Review Time: We aim to respond to all refund requests within 1 business day and complete the review within 3–5 business days.

Payment Processing:

  • bKash / Nagad / Rocket: 1–3 business days after approval
  • Debit/Credit Card: 5–10 business days (depends on your bank's processing time)
  • Account Credit: Applied immediately upon approval

Currency: Refunds are issued in BDT (Bangladeshi Taka) at the same rate paid. MenuQR is not responsible for exchange rate differences if you converted from another currency.

Disputes: If you believe a charge was made in error or you were charged after cancellation, contact us immediately. We take billing disputes seriously and resolve them promptly. Please contact us before initiating a chargeback through your bank — chargebacks result in fees for both parties and we prefer to resolve issues directly.
