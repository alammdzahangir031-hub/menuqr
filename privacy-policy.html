1

Information We Collect

When you use MenuQR — whether as a restaurant owner or as a customer placing an order — we collect certain information to provide and improve our service.

Information from Restaurant Owners (Account Holders):

  • Name, phone number, and email address provided at registration
  • Restaurant name, address, and business details
  • Menu content including item names, descriptions, prices, and photos you upload
  • Payment information processed through our third-party payment partners (we do not store card details)
  • WhatsApp number designated for receiving kitchen orders
  • Login credentials (password stored in encrypted form — we cannot read it)

Information from Customers Placing Orders:

  • Table number associated with each order
  • Items ordered, quantities, and special instructions
  • Device type and browser used to access the menu (automatically collected)
  • Approximate time and date of each visit

Automatically Collected Technical Data:

  • IP address and general geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Browser type, operating system, and screen size
  • Pages visited, time spent, and click activity within the platform
  • QR code scan logs (time and table number only, no personal identification)
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for specific, legitimate purposes only. We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

  • To provide the service: Process orders, send WhatsApp notifications to the kitchen, and display menus to customers
  • To manage your account: Authentication, billing, and account settings
  • To improve MenuQR: Understand how the platform is used, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To send service communications: Important updates about your account, system maintenance, and security alerts
  • To provide customer support: Respond to your questions, troubleshoot issues, and process refund requests
  • To generate analytics: Provide restaurant owners with aggregated order data, peak hours, and bestseller reports
  • Legal compliance: Meet our obligations under Bangladesh law and respond to lawful government requests

We will never use your information for purposes beyond what is described here without your explicit consent.

3

Information Sharing

MenuQR does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

With Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party companies to operate MenuQR. These include cloud hosting providers, payment processors (bKash, Nagad, card processors), WhatsApp Business API providers, and analytics tools. These providers access only the data necessary to perform their functions and are contractually bound to protect it.

Between Restaurant and Customer: When a customer places an order, the order details (items, table number, special notes) are shared with the restaurant owner's WhatsApp number. No customer contact information is collected or shared.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by Bangladesh law, court order, or a lawful request from a government authority. We will notify you where legally permissible.

Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of MenuQR, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before this occurs and your rights will be maintained.

✅ We never share your data with advertisers. MenuQR is an ad-free platform and our business model is subscription-based, not data-based.

4

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  • All data transmitted between your browser and MenuQR is encrypted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — not even our team can read your password
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and backups are performed
  • Payment card data is never stored on MenuQR servers — it is handled entirely by our PCI-DSS compliant payment partners

Data Retention: We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Order history is retained for 2 years to support analytics and dispute resolution. If you close your account, your personal data will be deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5

Cookies & Tracking

MenuQR uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and improve performance. We use the following types:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function (login sessions, language preference, cart state). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how the platform is used (page views, feature usage). You may opt out.
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use cookies for ad tracking or retargeting. Ever.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use MenuQR.

6

WhatsApp Integration

MenuQR uses WhatsApp to deliver order notifications to your kitchen. This is central to how the service works. Please note:

  • When you provide your WhatsApp number, it is used solely to receive automated order messages from MenuQR
  • WhatsApp messages are delivered via Meta's WhatsApp Business API. Meta's own privacy policy applies to the WhatsApp platform itself
  • We do not access your WhatsApp contact list, messages, or any other data beyond what is needed to send order alerts
  • Order messages include: table number, items ordered, quantities, special instructions, and order ID. No customer personal data is included

Your Rights

As a MenuQR user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON)
  • Right to Object: Opt out of non-essential data processing such as analytics
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@menuqr.com.bd. We will respond within 14 business days.

Children's Privacy

MenuQR is a business platform intended for use by adults (18 years and older) operating restaurants. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has submitted information to MenuQR, please contact us at hello@menuqr.com.bd.
