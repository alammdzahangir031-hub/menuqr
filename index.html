FAQ

Every Question Answered

Do customers need to download an app?

+

No. Customers just scan the QR code with their phone camera (built-in on all modern phones). The menu opens instantly in their browser. Zero friction, zero downloads.

How does the kitchen receive orders?

+

The moment a customer places an order, our system sends an automated WhatsApp message to your kitchen number with the full order details — table number, items, quantities, and special requests.

Can I update my menu anytime?

+

Yes, anytime. Login from any device, make changes in 10 seconds, and they go live instantly. Change prices, mark items sold-out, add daily specials — all in real time.

What if I have multiple floors or 50 tables?

+

No problem. We generate one unique QR code per table. We support unlimited tables across all plans. Each QR automatically sends the table number with every order.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

+

Zero contract. Cancel from your dashboard with one click, anytime. No cancellation fees. Ever.

Can different menus show at different times?

+

Yes. Schedule items to auto-show/hide by time. Set breakfast items to disappear at 11am, lunch menu to activate at 12pm. All automated.

Is my customer data safe?

+

AES-256 encryption (same as banks). We never sell your data. You own all customer data and can export it as Excel anytime.

Do you support Bangla?

+

Yes! Our platform is fully bilingual. Menu, customer interface, and owner dashboard all support Bangla and English. Our support team speaks Bangla.

Is bKash payment mandatory?

+

Not at all. bKash and Nagad payment is completely optional. You can use MenuQR for ordering only, and still accept cash as usual. Payment integration is an add-on.

What happens after my free trial?

+

We'll remind you 3 days before trial ends. Choose a plan and continue seamlessly. If you don't choose, your account pauses (not deleted) and you can resume anytime.
